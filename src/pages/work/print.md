## Print

Here are some projects that ended up on physical media, usually on paper.

<div class="gallery" markdown="1">

- ![Data Proposal Booklet](..//images/cradle-data-proposal.jpg)
    - Client wanted to produce a proposal for a data warehousing system to an international energy company. I was consulted to provide a proposal design that fit the scope and vision of the project. I provided overall document design, assessments of budget and man-hours for the project itself, and designed all infographics.
    - Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign
    - Colors: #000 #fff
- ![Cradle Training Catalog](..//images/cradle-training-catalog.jpg)
    - I designed the covers and created a visual style to be used throughout the book.
    - Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign
    - Colors: #000 #fff
- ![Cradle Employee Handbook](..//images/cradle-employee-handbook.jpg)
    - While employed at Cradle Solution, I took the opportunity to refresh the company's Employee Handbook. I redesigned the booklet covers to reflect Cradle brand desires of the period: Clean, mostly white design punctuated with color accents matching company logo. A matching style template was produced and applied to the booklet content.
    - Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Microsoft Word
    - Colors: #000 #fff
- ![Oxonoco Product Catalog](..//images/oxonoco-catalog.jpg)
    - Oxonoco wanted a fresh, modern look for their corporate profile. Taking elements from the corporate logo, I was able to supply a modular, flexible design that could work with multiple layout configurations.
    - Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign
    - Colors: #000 #fff
- ![Cradle Letter Ad](..//images/cradle-letter-ad.jpg)
    - Cradle Solution needed a full-page US letter-sized ad for a trade show that listed all the services their company provides. With exception of tagline, copy was 100% provided by client.
    - Tools: Adobe Photoshop
    - Colors: #000 #fff
- ![CradleMRx Promotional Fliers](..//images/cradlemrx-flyers.jpg)
    - In 2011, I created a series of promotional fliers for sales staff promoting CradleMRx medical software.
    - Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Blender
    - Colors: #000 #fff
